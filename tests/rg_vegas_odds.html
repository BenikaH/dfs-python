Player Prop Bets

Player prop bets show the value for a given category that Vegas projects a player to output in tonight’s game. These can be helpful in finding daily fantasy steals or studs on a given night.

LEGEND:
Total Prop (cat) – Vegas Prop Bet Line for that Category
ML (Over) – Money Line that the Player goes OVER that Prop Bet Line (More Negative values mean a player is more likely to go over)
ML (Under) – Money Line that the Player goes UNDER that Prop Bet Line (More Negative values mean a player is more likely to go under)
